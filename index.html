2D Generative World with Biomes

By Simon Katzer

About | Source

Move around the world with the arrow keys. Press D to toggle debug info. Press H to kill/resurrect your framerate by toggling the more detailed background. To start over with a new seed, reload the page.

There are four different biomes to explore, differentiated by their background color and wall characteristics. It is possible to get stuck in small corridors, as well as to spawn inside of a large wall; I did not have time to address these issues completely.

I've always been fascinated by terrain generation in video games. Three games in particular - Minecraft, Terraria and Civilization V - showed me the possibilities that emerge when game worlds are generated procedurally rather than hard-coded, and these games inspired me to use generative terrain in a project of my own. My goal was to generate simple 2D worlds composed of walls and open spaces for the player to explore, with several distinct biomes, and while the difficulties I encountered in implementing this in a JavaScript context took up most of my time, the end result is more or less in line with my expectations.
